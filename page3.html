Daniela Pagliarello (BFA York University 2009) has performed on many stages across Canada for audiences of dance, theatre and comedy, garnering a Best Actress nomination for her lead role in The Resistible Rise of Artuo Ui with the Red Light District Theatre Co.

Her first webseries FOOD N' MONEY caught the attention of the Toronto underground comedy scene and was reviewed as "The Broad City on Acid". She founded Food n' Money Productions which produces sketches. Her second series SHITTY ROOMMATE is due later this year. Daniela is an actor, writer, director, producer and show runner, currently in development of her third series EVIL HAGS.

Daniela recieved her training as an actor from York University as a student of their distinguished Acting Conservatory, having previously trained as a company dancer for 15 years. She lives in Toronto.
